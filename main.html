Payment

    We accept payment in any way, choose for yourself a convenient option:

  • Cash to courier
  • Online, Visa / MasterCard / AMEX / Union Pay
  • Card to the courier (the courier will bring the POS terminal and give you a check)
  • By bank transfer (for legal entities)

If you have changed the amount when paying:

Perhaps this is due to the availability of weighted goods in the order. Since it is not always possible to gain the exact weight up to a gram, the order value may slightly change as a result.

If the amount turned out to be less, and you have already paid online, you will see the changed amount charged in the statement within 3 business days.

Important! How is the amount returned:

1. The return of the amount does not occur by replenishment of funds on the card - the amount of the write-off itself on the day of placing the order changes.

2. If you have installed SMS banking, you will receive an SMS about blocking the amount immediately after registration and you will see a blocked amount until the actual weighing of the goods. If the total amount charged is less, the call center will warn you about this before leaving the courier.
